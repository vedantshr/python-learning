Why Dubai?


Located on the eastern coast of the Arabic Peninsula, Dubai is popularly known for its world-class infrastructure, high-tech facilities, and extravagant luxuries. If that doesn’t sound convincing enough to buy a property in Dubai, its strategic location, open economic policies, and efficient real estate laws are sure to blow your mind. Read on to know why Dubai is often an investor’s first choice to buy a property and invest therein. Is it the city’s old-world charm or the string of modern skyscrapers that lure the investors? Here’s why people love to invest in Dubai, and you should too.

Reasons why you must invest in Dubai

Some key factors that can't be ignored!!!!


Currency Appreciation

The US dollar holds the strongest economic position. Investing in Dubai is equivalent to investing in US dollars as the exchange rate between the US dollar and Dubai’s AED is fixed. It doesn’t fluctuate.

Guaranteed Home Loans

If you’re looking forward to investing in a property in Dubai, you can opt for a home loan which is guaranteed with no paperwork involved. This loan is generally available at around 3-4%. So, even if you rent out your property after taking a loan, you can make money with the bank’s money. That’s the beauty of investing in a property in Dubai.

0% Tax

If you invest in Dubai, you not only get a higher rental, but there is no income tax also on the rental income you earn. This surely is like a cherry on the cake. No wonder Dubai is a hub for global investors!
